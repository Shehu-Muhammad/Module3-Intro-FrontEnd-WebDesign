Beginner Bodyweight Workout

This is the Beginner Bodyweight Workout (3 Circuits)

  1. 20 Bodyweight Squats
  2. 10 Push-ups
  3. 10 Walking Lunges (each leg)
  4. 10 Dumbbell rows (use a milk jug or other weight)
  5. 15 Second Plank
  6. 30 Jumping Jacks
