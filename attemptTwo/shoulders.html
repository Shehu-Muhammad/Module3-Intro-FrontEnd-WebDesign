Shoulder Workouts

  1. Standing Dumbbell Fly

    1. Hold a dumbbell in each hand by your sides.
    2. Without shrugging, use your upper body to swing the weights up a few inches.
    3. Your arms and torso will form an upside down V shape.
    4. Think of it as a lateral raise with momentum but without full range of motion.

  2. Face Pull

    1. Attach a rope handle to the top pulley of a cable station.
    2. Grasp an end in each hand with palms facing each other. Step back to place tension on the cable.
    3. Pull the handles to your forehead so your palms face your ears and your upper back is fully contracted.

  3. Seated Dumbbell Clean

    1. Hold a dumbbell in each hand and sit on the edge of a bench.
    2. Keeping your lower back flat, lean forward.
    3. Explosively straighten your body and shrug the weights so your arms rise.
    4. Allow the momentum to flip your wrists so you catch the weights at shoulder level.

  4. Dip

    1. Rest the palms of your hands on a bench or chair, and, if available, place your heels on another elevated object in front of your so your legs are suspended.
    2. Lower your body until your upper arms are parallel to the floor.

  5. Dumbbell Neutral Grip Overhead Press

    1. Hold a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder level with palms facing each other and elbows pointing forward.
    2. Brace your core and press the weights straight overhead.
    3. At the top, shrug your shoulders and hold for a second.
